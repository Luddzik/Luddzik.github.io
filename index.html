ECIO MOBILE

GAME WORLD

Immerse yourself in the vast cosmos of ECIO Space set in stunning galaxy, where players get to battle and explore the world through various missions and challenges. There are different modes, involving different environments, containing unique rewards to enhance players gameplay experience.


ENGAGING GAMEPLAY

Explore the world of ECIO, going against the odds, and beat the missions in the Player versus Environment (PvE) mission, boss, or survival modes, each battle expanding on the world story and rewarding you with progression, items, and characters. For those seeking competitive gameplay, venture into the intense Player versus Player (PvP) battle mode, where skill and ingenuity will determine who reigns supreme.


UNIQUE CHARACTERS

Players will collect and assemble modular components to create one-of-a-kind characters that form their ultimate team. Mix and Match character components that suits your team composition and your prefered playstyle. With an unending combination of character customization and team formation tactics, ECIO Space offers a fresh and dynamic gaming experience.
